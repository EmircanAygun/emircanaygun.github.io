About

I was born and raised in Kocaeli, Turkey and have been passionate about drawing and sculpture since I was young. I’m a self-taught artist and still continue to learn and evolve. Exploring different techniques, styles, and materials in art is something I truly enjoy. My focus is always on creativity and aesthetics. Currently, I primarily work with watercolor, ink and charcoal.

I also recently graduated from the Computer Engineering program at Kocaeli University. I have a strong interest in Mobile App Development. I mainly work with Java and Kotlin, and I enjoy building Android applications using modern design patterns such as MVVM. I also have experience with C, Python, PHP, HTML/CSS, and MySQL, and I am familiar with cloud technologies. You can check out my projects on my GitHub profile.
